Printer Qualification Wizard

This wizard will generate a series of parts which, when printed, can be used to determine the minimum achievable feature size, as well as other important print characteristics.


Since we don't assume you have easy access to fancy metrology equipment, the wizard works iteratively: Print a part, then return here and answer a few questions about how it turned out, and the wizard will use that information to generate a new test part for you to print. After a few iterations, your printer's capabilities will be known with several digits of precision.
