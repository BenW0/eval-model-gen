Printer Qualification Wizard

This wizard will generate a series of parts which, when printed, can determine the smallest size features producible by the printer.


Since we don't assume you have easy access to fancy metrology equipment, the wizard works iteratively. For example, to find the smallest printable column,

The wizard will generate a part based on your printer's specifications.

Print that part, and look at how many columns printed

The wizard takes your response, and generates a new test part with a narrower range of columns

Print the new part, and look at how many columns printed

After a few iterations, your printer's capabilities will be known with several digits of precision.
